The WALK-A-THON program commencement, with the aim of increasing public awareness against drug addiction.

Publication Date: September 18, 2022

The WALK-A-THON program commenced in Dhangadhi, the heart of Sudurpaschim with the primary objective of raising public awareness against drug addiction. The event witnessed an enthusiastic participation of people from all walks of life, united in their determination to combat the pressing issue of drug abuse.

The program was organized on a grand scale and received immense support from schools,colleges, different organizations, and community members.

The event concluded with a closing ceremony, where Hon. Minister Deuba delivered a powerful speech, reiterating the significance of collective action in combatting drug addiction. He praised the determination displayed by the participants and vowed to continue supporting initiatives that promote public health and well-being.

The program's route was strategically planned to cover key areas in Sudurpaschim, maximizing its visibility and impact. Along the way, Hon. Minister Deuba interacted with local residents, distributing informational pamphlets, and encouraging them to actively participate in the fight against drug addiction.
